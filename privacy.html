Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 23, 2026

1. Overview

This website (fadiopgenorth.com) is operated by Fadi Opgenorth ("we," "us," "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle information when you visit this website, subscribe to our newsletter, submit a contact form, or book a call through our scheduling tool.

We do not sell, rent, license, or otherwise distribute your personal information to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes. Data collected through this site is used exclusively for internal business purposes — specifically, to respond to your inquiries, deliver newsletter content you have opted into, and facilitate scheduling of calls you have requested.

2. What We Collect

  • Contact form submissions: Name, email address, interest category, and message content submitted through our contact form.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Email address provided when subscribing to our newsletter.
  • Scheduling data: Name, email address, and any information you provide when booking a call through our scheduling tool (Cal.com).
  • Automatically collected data: Standard web server logs maintained by GitHub Pages, which may include IP addresses, browser type, referring pages, and timestamps. We do not control or access GitHub's server logs directly.

We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics purposes. We do not run any analytics tools (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.) on this site.

3. How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used strictly for internal purposes:

  • To respond to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • To send newsletter content to subscribers who have opted in
  • To facilitate and confirm scheduled calls
  • To improve our services and communications

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, ad targeting, or any purpose beyond the specific interaction you initiated.

5. Data Storage

We do not store personal data on this website. This is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages with no server-side database, no backend application, and no local data storage. All personal data you submit flows directly to the third-party processors identified above and is stored on their infrastructure, subject to their respective security practices and policies.

6. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions: Retained in our email systems for as long as necessary to respond to and resolve your inquiry, then archived or deleted at our discretion.
  • Newsletter subscriptions: Retained by Buttondown until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
  • Scheduling data: Retained by Cal.com according to their data retention policies.

7. Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing fadi@fadiopgenorth.com
  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you

We will respond to data requests within 30 days. Note that we can only delete data within our direct control — for data held by third-party processors, you may need to contact them directly or we will make reasonable efforts to facilitate your request.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data through this site, contact us immediately at fadi@fadiopgenorth.com and we will delete it.

9. International Users

This site is operated from the United States. If you are accessing this site from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. By using this site, you consent to such transfer and processing.

10. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, we shall not be liable for any unauthorized access to, or alteration, theft, or destruction of, personal data transmitted through or stored by third-party services used in connection with this site. We make no warranties, express or implied, regarding the security of any data transmitted over the internet or processed by third-party platforms.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of this site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
